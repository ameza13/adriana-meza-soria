Research

My research interests lie at the intersection of Software Engineering and Artificial Intelligence. I investigate how to integrate Generative AI into software development workflows by exploring novel ways to create high-quality synthetic data for code LLMs, AI software agents, and LLM powered systems. In previous years, I studied software engineering practices, centering on the analysis of software development meetings. I keep actively contributing to this line of research too.
