Research

My research lies at the intersectin of Software Engineering and other research areas, such as HCI and Artificial Intelligence. In previous years, I studied human aspects of software engineering in relation to design and software maintenance meetings. More recently, I have engaged on studying how to use Generative AI to improve software productivity. For this work, I have focused on the creation of synthetic data generation tools, code datasets, and AI sofware engineering agents.
