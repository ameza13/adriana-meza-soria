Research

My research lies at the intersection of Software Engineering with research areas such as HCI and AI. In previous years, I studied human aspects of software engineering centering on the analysis of design and software maintenance meetings. More recently, I have engaged on investigating the application of Generative AI to software development workflows with the goal of improving productivity.
